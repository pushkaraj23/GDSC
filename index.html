About Us

Concept of GDSC

The GDSC program is a grassroots channel through which Google provides development, mobile & web development skills for students, towards empolyability.

Why GDSC?

GDSC activities are targeted at University students and any others including faculty members who want to learn development skills & work to solve real life problems.
